title: Level of a Hilbert modular form
authors:
    jvoight
    lassina

The **level** of a Hilbert modular form $f$ is a nonzero integral ideal $\mathfrak{N}$ that determines the group $\Gamma_0(\mathfrak{N})$ under which $f$ transforms. 

The **level norm** is the absolute norm of the level of the form.
