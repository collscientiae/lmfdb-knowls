The **Riemann hypothesis** is the assertion that if $\rho$ is a zero of an 
analytic <a knowl="lmfdb/lfunction">L-function</a> then $\mathrm{Re}(\rho)>0$ implies 
that $\mathrm{Re}(\rho)=1/2.$
